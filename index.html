Introduction to Football

Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is a popular sport played and enjoyed by millions around the world. It is a team sport that involves two teams of eleven players each, trying to score goals by getting a ball into the opposing team's net.

Famous Football Teams

  • FC Barcelona
  • Real Madrid
  • Manchester United
  • Bayern Munich
  • Juventus

Basic Rules of Football

  1. The game is played in two halves, each lasting 45 minutes.
  2. Players are not allowed to use their hands or arms, except for the goalkeeper within their penalty area.
  3. Scoring a goal earns a team one point.
  4. The team with the most goals at the end of the game wins.
